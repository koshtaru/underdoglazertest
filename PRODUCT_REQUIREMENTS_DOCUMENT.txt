1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

1.1 PRODUCT VISION
The Underdog Lazer website serves as the primary digital presence for a 
professional laser engraving business, showcasing precision craftsmanship,
diverse service offerings, and exceptional quality to attract and convert
potential customers.

1.2 BUSINESS OBJECTIVES
- Generate qualified leads through contact form submissions
- Showcase portfolio quality to establish credibility
- Educate customers about laser engraving capabilities
- Provide seamless user experience across all devices
- Establish strong online brand presence

1.3 TARGET AUDIENCE
PRIMARY: 
- Corporate clients seeking branding and identification solutions
- Small businesses needing promotional materials
- Individuals wanting personalized gifts and custom items

SECONDARY:
- Industrial clients requiring precision marking
- Event planners seeking custom awards and signage
- Designers and agencies looking for manufacturing partners

1.4 SUCCESS METRICS
- Monthly contact form submissions: Target 50+ leads
- Session duration: Average 2+ minutes
- Pages per session: 3+ pages
- Mobile usage: 60%+ of total traffic
- Gallery engagement: 70%+ of visitors view gallery
